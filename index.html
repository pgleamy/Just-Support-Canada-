Local & Private: The application runs entirely on your local machine to ensure strict client data privacy.

1. Spousal Support (SSAG) Engine

  • Determine the precise Low, Mid, and High ranges for spousal support quantum and duration under the Spousal Support Advisory Guidelines.
  • Automatically applies the "With Child Support" or "Without Child Support" formula based on family composition.
  • Calculates Individual Net Disposable Income (INDI), allowing you to justify support ranges based on net income equalization.

2. Child Support Calculation (Section 3 & 7)

  • Calculates the base child support (Table Amount) using current and historical Federal Child Support Guidelines tables.
  • Handles complex residency arrangements including Shared (50/50), Split (one child each), and Primary care, with automatic set-off calculations.
  • Determines the proportionate sharing ratios for Section 7 Special Expenses based on the parties' respective incomes.

3. Dynamic Child Support Arrears Calculator

  • Automates the calculation of retroactive child support obligations and arrears.
  • Change of Circumstance: Handles adjustments to income, residency, or province of residence at any specific date within a tax year.
  • Generates a detailed audit trail that nets gross obligations against historical payments to determine the final outstanding balance.

4. Spousal Support Restructuring Tool

  • Step-Down Agreements: Design custom payment schedules that change over time (e.g., higher support now, stepping down in future years).
  • Visual Timeline: Build the agreement visually with automatic milestones synced to key dates like retirement or a child turning 18.
  • Benchmarking: Set your own "Target" deal (Amount & Duration) and instantly compare your structured proposal against it to see the precise difference in value.

5. Lump Sum Spousal Support

  • Calculates the Net Present Value (NPV) for a one-time cash buyout of spousal support.
  • Includes specific adjustments for tax consequences, mortality risk (contingency), and the time-value of money.
  • Identifies the "Settlement Zone" by calculating the Recipient's "Floor" value and the Payor's "Ceiling" cost.

6. Income Adjustments & Reporting

  • Supports Schedule III adjustments (e.g., Union dues, employment expenses) and automatically calculates dividend gross-ups.
  • Generates comprehensive Audit Reports displaying a full breakdown of income, taxes, and government benefits used in every calculation.

7. Case Management & Data Persistence

  • Save & Load: Saves complete scenario data to your local hard drive, allowing you to manage complex case histories securely.
  • Create unlimited "Scenario" files (e.g., "Scenario A" vs. "Scenario B") to compare different negotiation positions without re-entering data.
